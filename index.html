Research

I'm interested in applying AI and machine learning techniques to solve problems in healthcare, particularly in medical image analysis, glucose forecasting for diabetes patients, and mental health detection from speech signals.

Blood Glucose Forecasting System for Diabetes Patients
Nidhi Bhatt
Bachelors Major Project (2024-2025)
  • Description: Developed a blood glucose forecasting system for diabetes patients using XGBoost with SHAP interpretability, including glycemic range classification. Trained and tested on T1Grenada Diabetes and Shanghai T1DM/T2DM datasets.
  • Outcome: Outperformed existing methods by 22-38% on key metrics (RMSE, MAE, MAPE) across multiple prediction horizons. Achieved 78.3% accuracy for hypoglycemia and 82.7% for hyperglycemia detection.

Depression Detection from Speech Using a Voting Ensemble Approach
Nidhi Bhatt, Amita Jain, M. Jain, S. Bhatt
IEEE CICT'24
paper
  • Description: Developed a novel voting ensemble ML model for depression detection from speech signals using OPENSMILE toolkit on DAIC-WOZ dataset.
  • Outcome: Achieved 78.69% accuracy and 76.36% F1-score, outperforming previous approaches.
Comparative Analysis of Hybrid Cryptosystems for Secure Image Encryption
Nidhi Bhatt
IEEE PKIA'24
paper
  • Description: Compared two hybrid cryptosystems (AES-256-GCM-SHA384 and ChaCha20-Poly1305-SHA256) with ECDH key exchange for secure image encryption.
  • Outcome: Achieved strong encryption quality with minimal image correlation and robust security features.
